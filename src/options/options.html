Vivida Help

Active Group

The active group determines which themes are available in the popup for quick switching. Only themes assigned to the active group will appear when you click the extension icon.

Popup Menu

Clicking the extension icon opens the popup where you can switch between themes in the active group. Use it to quickly cycle through your curated themes without opening this page.

Drag and Drop

Themes can be dragged from the Unassigned Themes sidebar into any group, between groups, and back to the sidebar. Drop a theme onto a group's theme list to assign it, or drag it back to unassign it.

Rename

Groups can be renamed by clicking the pencil (✏) icon next to the group name. Type a new name and press Enter or click Confirm to save, or press Escape or click Cancel to discard.
