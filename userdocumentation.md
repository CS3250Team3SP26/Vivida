Setup:
1. Open up firefox on your laptop/pc.
2. Click in the search bar at the top.
3. Type in "about:debugging" then press enter.
4. Click on "This Firefox" on the left hand side.
5. Click on "Load Temporary Add-on..."
6. Navigate to the folder that contains your manifest.json file (usually in your src folder inside the github repository)
7. If you can't find the right folder, you can click on "This PC", double click on your main drive, click in the search bar, then type in "manifest.json".
8. Double click on "manifest.json" or click on it once then click on "Open" if you searched for this file using the search bar.
9. Click on "Reload" near the bottom right of the extension text window.

Usage:
1. Click on the extension symbol for firefox (it looks like a puzzle piece) near the top right corner of firefox.
2. Click on the extension labeled "Vivida".
3. Now you can select one of the default firefox themes by clicking on them or you can click on "Manage Groups".
4. Clicking on "Manage Groups" takes you to a new page in firefox which allows you to create new groups of themes, delete groups, add themes to different groups, or rename groups.
5. To create a new group; click on the "+" symbol, enter any name for that group, then click on "OK".
6. To delete a group; click on the red text "Delete" button.
7. To rename a group; click on the small pencil symbol next to the group name, enter a new name, then click on "Confirm".
8. To add themes to different groups, simply hold left click on any theme, and drag it to any group, then let go of left click; in other words simply drag and drop themes into any group to move them to a different group.
9. Any themes downloaded onto your pc/laptop are in the "UNASSIGNED THEMES" list on the left side of the Manage Groups page.
10. To download more themes, go to this website: https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/themes/, click on a theme, click on "Install Theme", then click on "Add" in the popup window in the top right of your screen.
